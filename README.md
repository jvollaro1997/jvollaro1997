## Hello! 👋

Julia Vollaro is a Graduate Student at Arizona State University. She completed her undergraduate in Film and Media Production with an emphasis in Editing. She is an Arizona native and currently a Career Technical Education Teacher for High School students. She teaches Film and TV Production and Digital Communication courses at Chandler High School. 
Before working in education, Julia worked four years at Warner Bros. Worldwide Television Marketing department as an assistant editor. She has been working freelance in the film and media industry for over 10 years with experience in the Camera and Editorial Departments. 

Julia is learning code to further her understanding of how humankind communicates via the Internet and to be able to write better expressions for animation in Adobe After Effects.
